Jana Antonissen

As a journalist Jana Antonissen (1992) is fascinated by the human stories behind the news. Hailing from Belgium, but based in Berlin she writes mostly for titles such as De Morgen and Humo.

Nadja Scherff

With a background in cultural and spatial sciences Nadja (1993) is intrigued by culture as an urban catalyst. She is based in Berlin where she studies a Master of Eastern European Studies at Freie Unversity.

Ksenia Les

As a photojournalist, Ksenia (1988) focuses on social issues in the countries of the former Soviet Union and explores a shift of the East-European identity beyond the stereotypes. Her work has been published in News Deeply, NEON, Taz.die Tageszeitung and Spiegel Online among others.

Bogdan Kolchenko

Having worked on international educational and fashion projects in Ukraine, Bogdan (1996) studies now in Berlin, though still following the cultural developments in his hometown Kyiv closely.
